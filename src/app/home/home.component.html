KETOS DASHBOARD

Welcome to the Ketos machine learning development and deployment platform. Ketos allows you to create your own development environment for R or TensorFlow and install all your needed packages. Once your environment ist installed you can request data from our data service and start building your own machine learning or statistical analysis model. Once your model is build it will be available for outside queries straight away, allowing it to be integrated as an expert system into apps or EHRs.

Is it your first time here? - check out our getting started guide Getting Started Guide
